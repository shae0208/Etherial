# Privacy Policy for Etherial

**Last Updated:** June 29, 2026

Thank you for using **Etherial**, a community-driven Discord bot designed to support competitive PvP players in *Etheria Restart* through build recommendations, team composition assistance, draft tools, comparisons, and other strategic resources.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Etherial collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## 1. Information We Collect

Etherial collects only the information necessary to provide its functionality and improve its services.

### Automatically Collected Information

When interacting with Etherial, the bot may collect and store:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Channel IDs where commands are used
* Timestamps of interactions
* Error logs and diagnostic information

### User-Submitted Information

Certain features may allow users to voluntarily submit information, including:

* Animus build recommendations
* Team compositions
* Counter suggestions
* Form submissions intended to improve Etherial's datasets
* Feedback, comments, or strategy recommendations

Only information intentionally submitted by users will be collected through these features.

## 2. How Information Is Used

Collected information is used solely to:

* Provide and operate Etherial's features.
* Generate build, comparison, team-building, and draft recommendations.
* Improve existing datasets and strategic recommendations.
* Analyze command usage to enhance bot functionality.
* Diagnose errors, bugs, or performance issues.
* Prevent abuse or malicious use of the bot.

Submitted data may be reviewed by the Etherial development team and incorporated into future updates.

## 3. Data Sharing

Etherial does **not** sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties.

Information may only be disclosed if:

* Required by applicable law or legal process.
* Necessary to protect the security, integrity, or operation of Etherial.
* Required to investigate abuse, exploitation, or violations of Discord's Terms of Service.

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

Data collected by Etherial is stored only for as long as necessary to operate, maintain, and improve the bot.

User-submitted strategy data may be retained indefinitely as part of Etherial's knowledge base unless removal is requested.

Logs and diagnostic information may be periodically deleted or anonymized.

## 5. User Rights

Users may request:

* Information regarding stored data associated with their Discord account.
* Correction of inaccurate submitted information.
* Deletion of personally identifiable information, where technically feasible.

Requests can be submitted to the Etherial development team through the project's official support channels.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Etherial operates on the Discord platform and is subject to the privacy practices and policies of Discord.

Users should also review:

* Discord Privacy Policy: https://discord.com/privacy
* Discord Terms of Service: https://discord.com/terms

Etherial is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially associated with XD Entertainment or Etheria Restart.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Etherial is not directed toward children under the age required to use Discord under applicable law. The bot does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect collected information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Etherial after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## 10. Contact

For questions, concerns, or data-related requests regarding Etherial, please contact the Etherial development team through the project's official Discord server or GitHub repository.
